# Radio Silent
Adventure Game for Introduction to Programming

## Game Overview
Radio Silent is a game where you play as a lone spaceman. You
are running low on stamina and fuel, and have a limited time to save
yourself. The goal is to survive, can you?

### World
The game takes place in space. All choices the player makes take place in the player's ship, and on planets they land on, The planet's environment depends on if said planet is viable for life. The player can find Food and Fuel, and a Relic. The items they find depend on whether the player is on their ship or on a planet.

### Characters
The only character in the game is you. Two out of the three endings do result in interaction with other creatures.

### Player Goals
The goal of the game is to unlock a positive ending, and survive.
